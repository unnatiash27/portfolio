About Me

Hello! I'm Unnati Singh, a passionate and detail-oriented full-stack developer with over two years of experience in building scalable, user-friendly web applications. I specialize in the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js) and have a strong background in front-end development with modern libraries like Tailwind CSS and frameworks like Next.js.

My journey in tech began with a curiosity for solving real-world problems through code, which led me to pursue a Bachelor's in Computer Science from VIT. Over the years, I’ve honed my skills by working on dynamic projects such as a real estate marketplace, a productivity app, and a blood donation platform, among others. My focus lies in writing clean, efficient code and delivering exceptional user experiences.

Beyond coding, I’m deeply passionate about design, data-driven solutions, and creative problem-solving. I thrive in collaborative environments and love brainstorming innovative ideas with like-minded individuals. When I’m not coding, you’ll find me exploring my interests in sketching, writing, and immersing myself in the artistry of Taylor Swift's music. My ultimate goal is to blend technical expertise with creative flair to build products that make a meaningful impact.

I’m always eager to learn new technologies and expand my skillset. Whether it’s diving deeper into machine learning, exploring cloud computing, or tackling new challenges in UI/UX design, I approach every opportunity with enthusiasm and a commitment to excellence.

Let’s connect and create something amazing together!
